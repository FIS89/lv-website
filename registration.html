LabelVision / IncrediBar Registration
Thank you for registering your LabelVision 98, LabelVision 2000, LabelVision 20/20, LabelVision Classic, or IncrediBar products with us.

For most versions of LabelVision, the serial number may be found by choosing the option About from the Help menu of the Designer or Print Only programs. For LabelVision Classic or IncrediBar, the serial number is printed on the Sentinel Key, which is a 2-inch square gray connector attached to your PC's parallel port. There are numbers on both sides of the Sentinel key: the serial number is the five-digit or ten-character string that is on the white sticker that says LabelVision or IncrediBar.
