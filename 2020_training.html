What type of training will be provided?

We will provide two separate sessions over two consecutive days: Basics & Advanced. Users who wish to learn the basics, then move to more advanced topics, should attend both sessions.

Session I – Basics

The first day of training is designed for users who are familiar with Windows applications but have little or no experience with LabelVision, or would like to further understand it.

Topics covered include: getting familiar with the Designer, Objects, Alerts, Fields, Data Sources, working with DBTools, when and how to use databases (basic level), using the Print Manager, designing Menus, creating LabelVision Projects, and much more.

Session II – Advanced

The Advanced training on the second day is designed for technical users who wish to learn more about LabelVision’s advanced features. We recommend attending the Advanced training only if you plan to attend the Basics session.

Topics covered include: Project design guidelines, using Macros and Formulas, Events, Variable Label applications, Active X Automation, advanced database design, integration with other systems, and much more.
