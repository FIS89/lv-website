LabelVision Classic: Year 2000: Upgrading From Version 2.3 or Earlier

LabelVision 20/20
If you are upgrading LabelVision Classic version 2.3 or earlier, and you use a printer supported by LabelVision 20/20 (HP, Epson and most Zebra, Intermec and DATAMAX printers), you should consider converting to LabelVision 20/20. LabelVision 20/20 is Year 2000 compliant, and offers unprecedented power and ease of use. See our special LabelVision 20/20 Offer for more information.

Free Upgrades to Version 2.4
If you purchased or upgraded to LabelVision Classic Version 2.3 after 4/15/98, then you are entitled to a free upgrade. You are also entitled to a free upgrade if you have Version 2.3 and you have an active maintenance contract with us. To receive your free upgrade:

  1. Get the list of serial numbers of all packages you own, which may be found on the Sentinel keys. There are numbers on both sides of a key; the serial number is the five digits or ten characters following "S/N:" on the side that says "LabelVision".
  2. Call us to verify eligibility for your upgrade, and we will send your free upgrade.
